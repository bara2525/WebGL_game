About the Game

Welcome to our thrilling game! The main objective is to defuse a bomb hidden in a city before the timer runs out. The bomb is concealed within a specific building, and it's up to the main character to locate and disarm it. As you navigate through the city, you will face hostile soldiers and traps set by the enemies. Stay alert, strategize your moves, and ensure you reach the bomb in time to save the day. Good luck!
